where you are now.

Whether you're entering the pool for the first time, returning after years away, training for fitness, or polishing technique — Swim-By offers structured beginner swim lessons and advanced programmes with supportive progression at every stage.

Adult Beginner Foundations

A calm, structured introduction to the water for adults who have never swum before or feel uncertain in the pool. We build breath control, buoyancy, and basic strokes at your pace.

Suits: First-time swimmers & returning adults

Water Safety & Confidence

Essential water awareness and survival skills for those who want to feel safe and confident in aquatic environments — pools, open water, or recreational swimming.

Suits: Anxious swimmers & safety-focused learners

Senior Wellness Swimming

Low-impact aquatic sessions designed to improve mobility, joint health, and cardiovascular wellbeing. A gentle, affirming environment for older adults seeking lifetime fitness swimming.

Suits: Older adults & mobility-focused swimmers

Stroke Technique & Performance

Precision coaching for swimmers looking to refine their freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, or butterfly. Video analysis and drill-based sessions sharpen form and build speed.

Suits: Intermediate to advanced swimmers
